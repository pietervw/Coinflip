Coin Flipping

Heads or tails? Coin tossing is the practice of throwing a coin in the air and checking which side is showing when it lands, in order to choose between two alternatives, heads or tails, sometimes used to resolve a dispute between two parties.

To play simply click/tap the coin. The flip result will display instantly! Your results will also be timestamped and saved in the browser and displayed in the History table.

Just Flip A Coin is perfect for:

  • Leaving simple yes/no decisions up to chance
  • Playing Heads or Tails with friends
  • Coin flipping to determine which of two teams start a match
  • Determining the winner of a tie
